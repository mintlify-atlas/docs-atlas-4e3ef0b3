SatDump includes pipelines for a broad range of non-weather missions — from geostationary maritime communication satellites and commercial launch vehicle telemetry to lunar probes and interplanetary spacecraft. Each accordion section below covers all pipeline IDs and configurable parameters for a given mission or satellite family.

<AccordionGroup>

  <Accordion title="Inmarsat">

    ### `inmarsat_std_c` — Inmarsat STD-C

    Processes the Inmarsat STD-C L-band data channel, which carries short text messages including SafetyNET maritime weather and distress messages.

    No additional parameters.

    ---

    ### `inmarsat_aero_6` — Inmarsat Aero 0.6k

    <Warning>This pipeline is a work in progress (WIP) and may not produce complete results.</Warning>

    Processes the Inmarsat Aero 0.6 kbit/s aeronautical satellite data unit channel.

    No additional parameters.

    ---

    ### `inmarsat_aero_12` — Inmarsat Aero 1.2k

    <Warning>This pipeline is a work in progress (WIP) and may not produce complete results.</Warning>

    Processes the Inmarsat Aero 1.2 kbit/s channel.

    No additional parameters.

    ---

    ### `inmarsat_aero_84` — Inmarsat Aero 8.4k

    Processes the Inmarsat Aero 8.4 kbit/s high-speed aeronautical data channel.

    No additional parameters.

    ---

    ### `inmarsat_aero_105` — Inmarsat Aero 10.5k

    <Warning>This pipeline is a work in progress (WIP) and may not produce complete results.</Warning>

    Processes the Inmarsat Aero 10.5 kbit/s channel.

    No additional parameters.

  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Stereo A/B">

    ### `stereo_lr` — Stereo-A/B Low Rate

    Processes the NASA STEREO (Solar TErrestrial RElations Observatory) low-rate science data downlink. Produces solar imager and in-situ particle data.

    No additional parameters.

    ---

    ### `stereo_hr` — Stereo-A/B High Rate

    Processes the STEREO high-rate data downlink, providing full-resolution imagery from the SECCHI and IMPACT instruments.

    No additional parameters.

  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="DSCOVR">

    ### `dscovr_tlm` — DSCOVR TLM Link

    Processes the NOAA Deep Space Climate Observatory (DSCOVR) telemetry link from the Sun–Earth L1 Lagrange point.

    No additional parameters.

    ---

    ### `dscovr_hr` — DSCOVR High-Rate Link

    Processes the DSCOVR high-rate downlink, providing EPIC full-disk Earth imagery and NISTAR radiometric data.

  <Accordion title="TUBSAT / TUBIN">

    ### `tubin_x_dump` — TUBIN X-Band Dump

    Processes the TU Berlin TUBIN (TUBSat-N) X-band data dump. Produces thermal infrared wildfire detection imagery.

    **Parameters:**

    - `check_crc` — Check frames for CRC errors and discard invalid frames. Disabling CRC checking may allow slightly more data to be decoded from a marginal pass, but at the cost of data integrity. Default: `true`.

  </Accordion>
